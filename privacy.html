🔒 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

1. Introduction

SoulHelper is a Discord bot designed for the SoulCord community. This Privacy Policy explains what information the bot stores and how it is used.

2. Information We Store

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Pokémon collection lists
  • Global collection lists
  • Shiny hunt selections
  • Rare and regional ping preferences
  • AFK preferences
  • Starboard configuration
  • Pokémon image hashes used for recognition

SoulHelper does not collect passwords, payment information, private messages, or email addresses.

3. How We Use Your Data

The stored information is used only to provide SoulHelper's features, such as Pokémon naming, collection tracking, shiny hunt notifications, rare and regional alerts, and server configuration.

4. Data Sharing

SoulHelper does not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties unless required by law.

5. Data Removal

Users can remove their collections, shiny hunts, and preferences using the bot's commands. Server administrators can remove server-specific settings through the bot.

6. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of SoulHelper means you accept the latest version.
