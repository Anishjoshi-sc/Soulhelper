Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

Introduction

SoulHelper is a Discord bot created for the SoulCord community. This Privacy Policy explains what information is stored and how it is used.

Information We Collect

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Collection lists
  • Shiny hunt selections
  • Notification preferences
  • Starboard settings
  • Image hashes for Pokémon recognition

SoulHelper does not collect passwords, payment information, email addresses, or private messages.

How We Use Data

Your data is used only to provide bot features such as Pokémon naming, notifications, collections, shiny hunts and server settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Information is only disclosed if required by applicable law.

Data Retention & Removal

Data is kept only as long as necessary for the bot to function. Users may remove their own collections, shiny hunts and preferences using the bot's commands where available.

Children's Privacy

SoulHelper is intended for users who meet Discord's minimum age requirements.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Changes & Contact

This policy may be updated periodically. Questions may be directed through the official SoulHelper support server when available.
